# Performance Management Module — STRIDE Evidence

## Scope and workflow

`DRAFT cycle → ACTIVE cycle → ASSIGNED review → FINALIZED review`

New cycles use questionnaire version `v2`. At assignment, the server resolves the employee’s applicable core, job-function, and leadership criteria, then encrypts an immutable questionnaire snapshot on the review. Existing cycles and reviews remain `v1` (five criteria / twenty indicators) and are neither silently converted nor rescored. Reopening preserves the assigned questionnaire version and snapshot.

HR Admin and HR Manager are normalized to the canonical `hr_manager` application role. They can manage Performance; a Regular Employee can read only their own finalized review. Other roles are blocked by the backend.

## v2 questionnaire and scoring controls

- All v2 reviews include nine core criteria (36 indicators): Attendance and Punctuality; Work Output and Productivity; Work Quality and Accuracy; Compliance, Safety, and Professional Conduct; Reliability and Accountability; Communication and Teamwork; Initiative and Problem-Solving; Adaptability and Continuous Development; and Job Knowledge and Technical Competence.
- The server conditionally adds one applicable Production, Logistics, Office, HR, Payroll, or IT section. Supervisor, Manager, and Executive classifications also receive leadership criteria. Unrelated sections are not combined.
- Ratings are `4 Exceeds Expectations`, `3 Meets Expectations`, `2 Partially Meets Expectations`, `1 Does Not Meet Expectations`, or `N/A`. N/A values are excluded from the average but require an encrypted reason. v2 requires at least two numeric ratings per criterion and numeric coverage of at least 75% of displayed indicators.
- Evidence and evaluator remarks are encrypted at rest. Remarks are required for N/A, low ratings, or below-standard criterion averages; all-4 criteria require evidence.
- Goals support status, measurement direction, target and actual values, confirmation, weight, evidence, and N/A reason. Their score is weighted by applicable rated goals. The default cycle weighting is `competency × 70% + goal achievement × 30%`; when goals are officially inapplicable, the cycle must use `100% / 0%` and that rule is stored in the snapshot.
- Finalization includes the questionnaire version and encrypted snapshot, ratings and N/A reasons, criterion evidence and remarks, goals, score snapshot, competency score, goal score, final weighted score, HR remarks, and development plan in its deterministic SHA-256 integrity payload. This is MySQL tamper-evident protection; it is not represented as blockchain-anchored unless separately anchored to Hyperledger Fabric.
- Below-standard outcomes require a structured development plan with performance gap, required action, responsible person, target date, follow-up date, and expected outcome. The module does not automatically terminate employees.
